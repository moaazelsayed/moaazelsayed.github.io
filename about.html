A little about me.
Where do I start? Well, When I was born...

When I was born, in the great and majestic mountains of Tuholok, a very unique and magical phenomenon occurred; It was foretold that it only happens once in a million years when a gifted child is born. Unfortunately, the gifted child was my neighbor who was also born on the same day. Jokes and stories aside, I'm a normal (somewhat creative) kid who has a strong knack for electrical and mechanical gadgets. Growing up, I always wondered how things were made from radios to microwaves, which resulted in almost every object in my house taken apart and (and some) put back together... most of the time without the slightest clue of how it works. As I got older I got introduced to the all famous "Interwebs" and online tutorials which helped in the advancement of my knowledge of how things worked and their creation.
